⑤开启远程访问

1.防火墙添加3306端口

查看firewall状态

firewall-cmd –state

状态是not running，启动firewall

systemctl start firewalld

状态是running

开放3306端口
firewall-cmd –zone=public –add-port=3306/tcp –permanent
重新载入
firewall-cmd –reload
